Services

Our offerings evolve alongside the markets we operate in. Below is our current flagship strategy.

Market-Neutral Arbitrage

We identify and capture pricing discrepancies between correlated markets. This strategy is deployed primarily in highly liquid environments with clear settlement criteria, such as regulated prediction markets. Our focus is on realizing spreads quickly and recycling capital efficiently.

  • Hedged positions with no directional exposure.
  • Fee-aware modeling and structured exits.
  • Strict risk limits on position and venue levels.
  • Designed for short-duration opportunities.
